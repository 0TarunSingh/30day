🌹 Day 17 – A Pocket of Sunshine

Today is about smiling and feeling light in your chest

💌 Introduction

"Today isn't about waiting. Today is about smiling, because even if you are away, Jiji, I still want you to feel light in your chest, like the world is giving you a little pocket of sunshine."

Letter to Jiji, from Volon

💖

My Jiji,

I thought about what I miss most when you are not here. It isn't just your words, or your replies, or the way you tease me sometimes. It's the way you make everything brighter, without even trying. You have this magic, where you just exist, and suddenly the world feels softer, kinder, lighter.

So today I don't want to write about waiting. I want to write about joy. I want to remind you of the silly things we've laughed about, the random little moments where you've made me grin like an idiot. Those moments are treasures to me, Jiji. They stay with me, even when you are away, and they remind me that love is not only about missing each other. It's about carrying the happiness we've built together and letting it warm us.

If you are reading this right now, I hope you smile. Even if it's small, even if it's just for a second. Because your smile is my favorite thing in the whole world. I live for it.

So here's my sunshine for you: I love you in every ordinary moment, and I love you in every extraordinary one too. You are my happiness, my Jiji, always.

Always yours,
Volon

✨ Quote of the Day

"You are my sunshine, Jiji, not because you make every day perfect, but because you make every day brighter just by being in it."
