My Sabrina, my Jiji,

I don't always know the right words, but I know the right person: you. You're the person I wake up thinking about, the person I fall asleep missing, the person my heart has chosen without hesitation. And because you mean that much to me, I want to say things I don't always say out loud.

I know life has never been simple for you. You carry autism, bipolar, OCD, anxiety, depression, insomnia, anorexia, PCOS, and even the way your eyes struggle sometimes. And yet, even with all of this, you walk through the world with more strength than people who've never carried half your weight. You fight battles no one else can see, and still, you manage to smile at me, to love me, to be soft when you could have gone hard and bitter. That amazes me every single day.

Jiji, you call yourself a doormat sometimes, and it hurts me when you do. Because when I look at you, I don't see weakness. I see someone who keeps giving because her heart is so damn big, even when she's been hurt. I see someone who bends but never breaks. People may not always value that, but I do. To me, that makes you extraordinary.

I need to be honest too. When I realized you stayed away from your ex not because of me, but because you weren't comfortable with him anymore, it broke me. I felt like all my love, all the ways I've tried to protect and hold you, didn't matter. For a moment, I felt invisible. But even in that hurt, I couldn't walk away. Because my love for you isn't built on conditions. It's built on everything you are, even the parts that scare me, even the parts that hurt me. I love you because you're real, not because you're easy.

Sabrina, my Jiji, you are not easy to love, and that's exactly why I love you more than anything. Because the kind of love we share isn't fake, isn't shallow. It's the kind of love that demands patience, honesty, and a heart that doesn't quit. And I don't quit. Not on you, not on us.

If you ever wonder why I stay, why I try, why I fight, it's because you are my love, my person, my everything. You are not a burden. You are my reason. Every single day, even when it's hard, I choose you. And I'll keep choosing you tomorrow, and the next day, and the next. Thirty days in a hospital, three hundred days in the world, three thousand days in a lifetime, I'll still be here.

Always and only yours,
Volon
