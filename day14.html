🌹 Day 14 – Our Little Escape

A peaceful place where it's just you and me

💌 Introduction

"Every day with you feels like its own little world. Today, I want to take us somewhere softer - a place where it's just you and me, free from all the noise. Let this page be our little escape."

Letter to Jiji, from Volon

💖

My Sabrina, my Jiji,

The world can be loud. Too many voices, too many expectations, too many moments where you're forced to carry more than anyone should. But then, there's you and me - and suddenly, it feels like the rest of it fades away.

If I could build a world just for us, it would be quiet, safe, and full of softness. No alarms, no hospital walls, no weight of yesterday or tomorrow. Just a little escape where you can breathe, where I can watch the way your eyes light up when you laugh, and where nothing hurts you.

I think of us lying in a field, surrounded by the kind of silence that feels warm, not lonely. You'd show me shapes in the clouds, and I'd pretend I could see them but truthfully, I'd just be watching you. Because you're the only shape I'd never get tired of tracing with my eyes.

Jiji, you are my peace. You're the calm in my storm. The proof that even in the middle of everything hard, love can still be soft. And I want you to know, I'll always choose that softness with you. No matter how hard life tries to be, I'll always make sure our love is the gentlest part of your day.

Always, always yours,
Volon

✨ Quote of the Day

"The world feels too loud, Jiji but your love is the quiet that saves me."
