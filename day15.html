💌 Introduction

"Half the month has gone, Jiji. And though these last few days felt quiet without you, my heart still found ways to speak to you. Even when you're busy, even when you're away, I still count my days with you because my love doesn't pause when you do."

Letter to Jiji, from Volon

💖

My Jiji,

These past few weeks have felt strange. The messages were slower, the space felt heavier, and I caught myself missing you more than usual. I know you were busy, I know life pulled you away and I kept reminding myself that it's okay. Because even when you can't be here, I still hold you close.

Day 15. Half the month. A mark on the calendar, but more importantly, a mark on my heart. It's proof that even with silence, distance, and waiting, my love for you keeps moving forward.

I won't lie I wish I could have spent this halfway point with you. I wanted to share the laugh, the joy, the moment. But instead, I share this truth: I am still here, waiting and loving, even when the days feel quiet.

Jiji, I hope when you read this, you know that these words don't fade with time. Whether it's day 15, day 20, or day 1000, my love is constant. And no matter how many times you're busy, or life steals you away, I'll be here, steady, halfway, all the way, always.

Yours even in silence,
Volon

✨ Quote of the Day

"Love doesn't keep time the way clocks do. Even when days pass in silence, my heart still counts you in every moment."
