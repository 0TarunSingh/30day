🌌 The end – When the World Shakes

Holding on even when I don't know how

💌 Introduction

"Today feels different, Jiji. The plan was supposed to be thirty days, but life never follows our plans. Things changed for you so suddenly, and my heart hasn't stopped racing with worry since I heard. This letter is me trying to hold on, even when I don't know how."

Letter to Jiji, from Volon

💖

My Jiji,

I don't know where to even start today. Everything feels terrifying. I thought I had thirty days to make something special for you while you were in the hospital, but now the world has shifted and you are out sooner, living somewhere else, dealing with so much more than I ever wanted you to. I feel like the ground is shaking under both of us, and all I want is to keep you safe.

I am scared, Jiji. Not of you, but for you. I know you are strong, I know you always find a way to survive, but the thought of you being kicked out, of you having to start over like this, it makes me want to hold you tighter than ever. And yet… I can't. You are avoiding me, and I don't know what to do with my head.

My BPD makes this part so much harder. My thoughts chase me in circles. I keep asking myself if I did something wrong, if I could have done more, if you are slipping away from me and I don't even know it. I feel my chest tighten with every silence. I suffer with these thoughts more than I can put into words, but the only thing that keeps me steady is how much I love you.

I don't know if you see it, but my love has no off switch. Even when it hurts, even when it scares me, it is still there. Maybe that is why it is terrifying, because losing you feels like losing the only safe place I have in this world. You are my heart, my home, the person I want to fight for no matter how broken I feel inside.

I am not asking you to fix me. I am just asking you to know that no matter where you are, hospital, your dad's house, even when you are avoiding me, I am still here. I cannot stop loving you. I cannot stop worrying. I cannot stop wanting a future where we are okay, together, stronger than all this chaos.

If all I can do is keep writing, then that is what I will do. Because my letters will always find you, even when I can't.

Always yours,
Volon
