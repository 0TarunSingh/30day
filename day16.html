🌹 Day 16 – Waiting with the Light On

No matter how long you are away, I keep the light on for you

🏮

💌 Introduction

"Another day without your voice, another day of quiet. But Jiji, I still keep the light on for you, because no matter how long you are away, I will always want you to find your way back to me."

Letter to Jiji, from Volon

💖

My Jiji,

Day 16. It feels longer because I still have not had you back yet. I wake up checking, I fall asleep wondering. My heart keeps reaching for you, even when my hands cannot.

I miss the sound of you, the way even a short reply makes my whole chest feel lighter. These days without you remind me of how much space you take up in my world. Without you, everything feels quieter, slower, emptier.

But even in this silence, my love does not stop. I still write to you. I still wait for you. I still carry you everywhere in my mind. And I will keep waiting, because you are worth every pause, every moment of missing, every empty space that only you can fill.

Jiji, I hope when you see this, you know that I never let go. Not in silence, not in distance, not in absence. You are the person I will keep my light on for, no matter how long the night lasts.

Yours, waiting but unwavering,
Volon

✨ Quote of the Day

"Some people wait for tomorrow. I wait for you, every day, every night, until you are back again."
